## 🧠 About Me
I am an MSc Artificial Intelligence for Biomedicine and Healthcare student at University College London (UCL), and a First-Class BSc Neuroscience graduate from Queen Mary University of London. My research interests focus on NeuroAI, computational neuroscience, and integrating machine learning with neural systems. 

## 💻 Featured Projects
* **Box Box Bot:** An autonomous F1-inspired driver trained using Proximal Policy Optimisation (PPO) reinforcement learning.
* **Predictive Modeling:** Preprocessing pipelines and evaluation of various ML models (including Logistic Regression, Random Forest, Gradient Boosting, and partially recurrent neural networks) using metrics like F1-score and Matthews Correlation Coefficient.
